About us

The Department of Computer Science under the banner of EXCEL proudly presents, ASCEND 2019, a national level technical fest to inspire hunger computer science geeks, and provide them a platform to show their excellence and talent in the technical field, is to be held on 9th and 10th of August. From its very first installment, ASCEND has set new benchmarks on the level of technical excellence on display every year. Always true to the standards, over the years ASCEND has accumulated such prestige that only few other fests in South India can boast of.

The newest instance promises to be even bigger and better. With the theme Autarquico, this year’s Ascend aims to highlight today’s autonomous world and move towards the future. This extravaganza unfolds to you a two-day mega event full of merry and rapture. Get enlightened on the redefining moments of technological brilliance.

The miracles of science.
